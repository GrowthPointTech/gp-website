She didn't know her voice yet. Didn't know her style. Didn't know how to translate her individual competence into collective impact.

**What I Do**

I partner with CEOs, senior leaders, and investors in high-growth, VC and PE-backed companies to design and build the people architecture that turns ambitious vision into lasting enterprise value.

My work spans three interconnected disciplines:

**Leadership Acceleration** — Identifying, assessing, and developing the high-potential middle managers who got promoted because they were exceptional individual contributors, and who now need to become the leaders their teams and companies deserve. This layer is the multiplier for everything. It is where culture either takes root or dies. Where strategy either gets executed or gets lost.

**Talent Architecture** — Designing the hiring models, role clarity, and organizational structures that attract, select, and retain the people who move the business forward. This is not just recruiting — it's building the system that ensures every hire advances the business.

**People Operations for Scale** — Building the people infrastructure, systems, and programs that support sustainable growth — reducing friction, reinvesting in high-impact areas, and creating an employee experience that scales with the business.

I deliver all of this through a coaching philosophy that is invitational, not directive. I create the conditions where leaders choose to grow, own their development, and build the self-awareness that makes them effective in environments that have no historical playbook — including the AI-native organizations defining the next generation of high-growth companies.
