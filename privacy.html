Privacy Policy

Who We Are

GrowthPoint Tech Advisors LLC (“GrowthPoint”) is a cybersecurity, risk and regulatory compliance consulting company that provides fractional CISO, cybersecurity, and other GRC consulting services to our clients. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

What Personal Data We Collect and Why

Contact Form

If you use our contact form, we collect the information you provide (such as your name, email address, and message) so we can respond to your inquiry. We do not use this information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly consent.

Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for basic site functionality. We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes. No personal data is collected from cookies for regular visitors.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you submit via the contact form solely to respond to your inquiry or provide customer support.

How We Share Your Data

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., website hosting providers, who are contractually obligated to protect your data).

How Long We Retain Your Data

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for our records, unless you request deletion.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@gptechadvisors.com.

How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
