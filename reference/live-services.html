ISMS Program

GrowthPoint takes a strategic view toward cybersecurity, governance, risk and compliance that is framework agnostic.  We can work with the major frameworks such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST CSF 2.0, and CIS Controls and help you determine which frameworks works best where you are on your cybersecurity journey.  The interactive graphic below shows the high-level components of a mature Information Security Management System (ISMS) program.  Click on each title in the graphic to see more details.

Leadership, Scope & Governance (LSG)

  • Establish Security Culture & Direction
  • Align Security Objectives with Strategic Goals
  • Establish the Scope of the Program
  • Ensure Competent Resource Allocation
  • Assign Relevant Roles
  • Executive Reporting on Program Effectiveness
  • Management Reviews

Policy & Documentation Governance (PDG)

  • Cyber Program Documented
  • Policy Governance Framework
  • Documentation Governance
  • Policies, Standards, Procedures, Frameworks

Risk & Compliance Framework (RCF)

  • Risk Assessment/Business Impact Analysis
  • Legal & Regulatory Compliance
  • Client Contractual Requirements
  • Data Privacy
  • Risk Register Tracking
  • Risk Treatment & Mitigation Controls
  • Third Party Vendor Management

Internal Security Controls (ISC)

  • Asset Lifecycle Management (Software & Hardware)
  • Identity Account Management & JML Processes
  • Data Protection & Access Control
  • Secure Configuration & Hardening
  • Malware Protection
  • Audit Log Management & SIEM
  • Physical Security
  • Email & Web Security Protections
  • Data Protection & Encryption
  • Backup & Data Recovery
  • Network Security, Monitoring & Defense
  • Continuous Vulnerability Management
  • Automated Penetration Testing
  • Application Software Security

Incident Management Response (IMR)

  • Incident Detection
  • Event & Incident Management
  • Identified Responsible Parties
  • Communication Plan
  • Formal, Tested Incident Response Plan
  • Lessons Learned Process
  • Cyber Breach Insurance
  • Documented Breach Reporting Requirements
  • Tabletop Exercises

Communication, Training & Awareness (CTA)

  • Security Awareness & Education
  • Security Training
  • Communication
  • Policy & Standard Education
  • Phishing Training

Availability & Continuity (AVC)

  • Monitoring & Uptime
  • Resiliency & Redundancy
  • ICT Readiness
  • Data Backups & Restoration
  • Business Continuity Planning
  • Disaster Recovery

Audits & Certifications (AUD)

  • Internal Controls Documented & Tracked
  • Internal Audit Program
  • External ISO 27001:2022 Certification
  • External SOC 2 Type II Audit with Evidence

Monitoring, Measuring, and Continual Improvement (MMC)

  • Identify & Address Nonconformities, incidents, and finding
  • Root Cause Analysis & Corrective Actions
  • Review & Update Internal Controls

Regulatory and Data Privacy Compliance

Navigating the ever-evolving landscape of data privacy and regulatory compliance can be complex, time-consuming, and high risk—especially for growing organizations handling sensitive information. At GrowthPoint, we help businesses confidently meet their obligations under a wide range of U.S. and international regulations, including:

  • GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act)

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

  • CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act)

  • HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)

  • State-specific AI and data privacy laws

 

Whether you’re preparing for your first regulatory audit, responding to new data subject access requests, or reassessing your internal controls to accommodate business changes, we deliver strategic guidance and practical implementation support.
