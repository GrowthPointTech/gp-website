About Us

Navigating the cybersecurity and compliance landscape can be very confusing and overwhelming without the right experience and guidance. At GrowthPoint, we use our experience to help our clients structure and strengthen their cybersecurity and compliance programs so that they can scale their business.

Our Mission

GrowthPoint was created by founders who have scaled companies and have a strong desire to help other companies structure, strengthen, streamline, and scale their companies for success. We leverage our decades of experience to help you – whether you are just starting out and need some free advice or starting to mature and scale and need more help, we can provide it. We help companies in the regulated finance and healthcare spaces from startups through small enterprises (Series B and C rounds).

Our Team

Stacey Robinson, CISSP, CHPS

Managing Partner, Fractional CISO

Stacey Robinson is a results-driven leader with experience in building and leading cybersecurity, enterprise risk and regulatory compliance programs. He excels in developing high-performing cybersecurity teams, maturing cybersecurity programs, driving strategic growth, and securing top industry certifications. Stacey collaborates effectively with boards, executive teams, and diverse stakeholders and is recognized for his ability to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical stakeholders, ensuring that cybersecurity, risk, and regulatory compliance initiatives align with business objectives.

Throughout his career, Stacey has held pivotal roles in both startup and growing companies, leading cybersecurity and product engineering organizations. As a trusted advisor and mentor, he supports executives and leadership teams in strategic planning for cybersecurity and technology, establishing strong foundations, maturing existing programs, mentoring team members, and fostering high-performing cultures.

Stacey holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The Citadel and a Master of Science in Computer Science from George Mason University. He also has CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) and CHPS (Certified in Healthcare Privacy and Security) certifications through ISC2 (International Information System Security Certification Consortium) and AHIMA (American Health Information Management Association) respectively.
