About Us

Built for the Security Challenges That Actually Keep You Up at Night

GrowthPoint Technology Advisors was founded on a simple belief: every organization deserves enterprise-grade cybersecurity — not just the ones with enterprise budgets.

Our Story:

Where GrowthPoint Came From

GrowthPoint was built by people who have scaled companies themselves — and who know firsthand how hard it is to build a credible security program when you're also trying to grow a business. Startups and growth-stage companies in regulated industries like finance and healthcare face the same security threats as large enterprises, but without the same resources to address them.

We started GrowthPoint to change that. Our fractional model gives companies from early-stage startups through Series B and C a senior security leader who is fully invested in their mission — without the cost of a full-time hire. The result is a right-sized security program that supports growth, earns customer trust, and holds up under audit.

Our Mission:

Scale Cybersecurity with Confidence

We help growing organizations build resilient security programs — structured, strengthened, streamlined, and ready to scale. Whether you're pursuing your first compliance certification or maturing an existing program, GrowthPoint gives you the expertise and momentum to get there.
